Academic Programs & Classes

Our school offers age-appropriate, concept-based learning from foundational classes to middle school level. We focus on literacy, numeracy, language development, general awareness, science learning, and personality development.

Pre-Primary (NUR, LKG, UKG)

Early childhood learning is designed to build confidence, communication, social interaction, and foundational reading and number skills through activity-based methods and playful learning.

Primary Section (I to V)

Students are guided through strong basics in English, Mathematics, Environmental Studies, Hindi, General Knowledge, and creative activities. We focus on understanding rather than rote memorization.

Middle Section (VI to VIII)

The curriculum becomes more structured with Science, Social Science, Mathematics, English, Hindi, and project-based learning. Students are encouraged to ask questions, analyze concepts, and improve communication.

Admission Information

Admissions are open for the current academic session. Parents are encouraged to visit the school campus, understand our academic approach, and complete the admission process on time.

Admission Process

  • Visit the school office or contact the admission help desk.
  • Collect or fill the admission inquiry/application form.
  • Submit student details and required documents.
  • Attend interaction/assessment if applicable for the class level.
  • Complete fee payment and confirm admission.

Required Documents: Birth certificate, previous report card (if applicable), passport size photographs, transfer certificate (if applicable), and parent contact details.

Why Parents Choose Our School

Parents look for trust, academic support, discipline, safety, and value-based growth. Our school is built around these essential expectations with a practical and caring approach.

At Techno School Of Innovation, we understand that school education forms the foundation of a child’s future. That is why we focus not only on completing a syllabus but also on making sure every child actually understands what they learn. Our teaching style encourages curiosity, clear concepts, classroom participation, reading habits, communication development, and respectful behavior.

In the early classes, children need confidence, routine, and emotional comfort. We therefore create a welcoming environment where children feel motivated to attend school and participate in class activities. In higher classes, students need clarity in subjects, exam preparation, time management, and guidance. Our teachers work consistently to improve these areas through explanation, revision, class tests, and feedback.

We also believe that education should help children become responsible human beings. Therefore, moral values, discipline, cleanliness, punctuality, kindness, teamwork, and respect are important parts of our school culture. This balanced approach supports long-term academic and personal success.
